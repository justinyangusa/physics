PhysicsBowl

The PhysicsBowl is a 45-minute, 40-question multiple-choice test offering two divisions: Division I for first-year students, and Division II for students that have already taken more than one year of physics and for anyone else looking for a challenge. The exam covers a broad range of high school physics topics including (but not limited to) mechanics, E&M, waves, light, optics, and modern physics. Scientific calculators are allowed and an equation/constants sheet will be provided during the test. More detailed information and past exams can be found on the PhysicsBowl website.

Congratulations to the Gunn students who were part of the PhysicsBowl team this year! Gunn placed second regionally in Division 1.
